Doctor Who is a British sci-fi TV program that first aired in 1963 and was rebooted in 2005. The Doctor, a Time Lord from the planet Gallifrey, travels through time and space in their time machine, the TARDIS, accompanied by various friends and companions.

The Matrix (or The Matrix of Time) is a Time Lord supercomputer that contains information on almost all known history in the universe, which allows prediction of future events. This Matrix of Time contains every episode of Doctor Who since the 2005 reboot, and stores user ratings and comments.
