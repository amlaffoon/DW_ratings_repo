Doctor Who is a British sci-fi TV program that first aired in 1963 and was rebooted in 2005. The Doctor, a Time Lord from the planet Gallifrey, travels through time and space with their friends and companions in their time machine the TARDIS.

The Matrix (or The Matrix of Time) is a Time Lord supercomputer that contains information on almost all known history in the universe, which allows prediction of future events.
